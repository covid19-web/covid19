COVID-19 Extraordinary Call for Proposals

Project title: Stop COVID`19

Concerning the implementation period from 01.07.2020 to 31.12.2020

The project has use case, implemented in Ukraine and Belarus. STOP COVID 19 activities will run from June 2020 to December 2020

The information about health and seek persons in Lviv region, Western Ukraine and Belarus will be collected and processed.

General information

Two countries will participate in the STOP COVID 19 project.

Ukraine is represented by Lviv Polytechnic National University and Danylo Halytsky Lviv National Medical University.

Belarus is represented by Belarus State Economic University.

Description of the activity

The implementation of our ideas will allow us to prove the patterns of dependence on the prevalence of COVID 19 in the territory of different countries.

Depending on the presence in humans of certain antibodies to influenza virus, tuberculosis, which may be correlated with a particular human blood group.

Results

The dataset for COVID19 resistance prediction is used.

The results will be published in European scientific journals and will be implemented on at the level of the project participants.

Selected core sector


Healthcare and telemedicine
